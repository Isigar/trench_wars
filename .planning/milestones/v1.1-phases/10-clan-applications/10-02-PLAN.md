| T-10-02-01 | Elevation of Privilege | apply() eligibility bypass | mitigate | All three guards (recruiting, not-already-member, no-duplicate-pending) are enforced inside apply(); controllers cannot opt out — the service is the sole write path (D-004) |
| T-10-02-02 | Tampering | duplicate-pending race between guard #3 and INSERT | mitigate | The partial unique index (plan 10-01) is the last-line defence; if two requests pass the guard concurrently, the second INSERT raises a unique violation rather than persisting a duplicate |
